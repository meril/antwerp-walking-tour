### Thematic Threads to Weave Through

1. **The Brabo Legend** — appears at Cathedral (Handschoenmarkt fountain), Grote Markt (larger fountain), and MAS (hand motifs on facade)

2. **Rubens' Antwerp** — Cathedral altarpieces, his home, Sint-Carolus Borromeus church he helped design, Groenplaats statue

3. **Trade & Prosperity** — Guild houses showing mercantile wealth, Scheldt River as source of fortune, port heritage at Het Eilandje

4. **Architectural Evolution** — Gothic (Cathedral, Vleeshuis) → Renaissance (City Hall) → Baroque (Sint-Carolus, Rubenshuis) → Neoclassical (KMSKA, Centraal Station) → Contemporary (MAS)

5. **Destruction & Survival** — Iconoclasm (1566), Spanish Fury (1576), French looting (1794), German occupation (1914) — and how art/buildings survived or were restored
